News app

My portfolio work involves the creation of a news website. Users can seamlessly browse the latest news on the homepage and access a comprehensive news feed on the dedicated news feed page, which includes all the news available in the database. For authenticated users, there is the additional functionality to contribute by adding news through the 'create topic' page and managing topics, including topic deletion. This project showcases my proficiency in web development and user-friendly news consumption.
