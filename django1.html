Web Store

For Users: Welcome to my Django web store project! Explore my wide range of items, view prices, descriptions, and captivating photos. Add items to your cart, sign up, and enjoy a streamlined checkout process. With user authentication, your information is securely stored for future visits. Update your profile with ease and make confident payments through our integrated Stripe API system.

For Store Staff: Manage your inventory effortlessly with our dedicated admin panel. Upload new products and modify existing listings seamlessly. Gain valuable insights into order management, track statuses (complete, shipped, or canceled), and ensure efficient coordination. Rest assured, the admin panel is accessible only to authorized staff, prioritizing security and protecting sensitive information.
